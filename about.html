About Us

Personal care for your healthy living

We have planned to make a web application named Medistant as a solution. It is an organized computerized system designed and programmed to deal with day-to-day operations of the hospital activities. The program can look after inpatients, outpatients, records of patients, billings in the pharmacy and labs. It also maintains information about specialised doctors. The major problem for the patient nowadays to get prescription and lab reports after consultation, many hospital managing reports in their system but it's not available to the patient when he / she is outside. In this project we are going to provide the extra facility to store the prescription and lab reports in the database and make available from anywhere in the world.
